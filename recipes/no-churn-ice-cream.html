No-Churn Ice Cream

A creamy No Churn Ice Cream that can be whipped up in minutes!

Ingredients

Base

  • 1 tin Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 1 Tbsp Vanilla Extract
  • 500ml Whipping Cream

Additions optional

  • Grated Chocolate
  • Roasted Nuts
  • Candied Fruits
  • Sweets or Candies
  • Crumbled Halva

Method

  1. Ensure all base ingredients are chilled. Chop or grate all additions into small pieces.
  2. Whisk base in a cake mixer until soft peaks form. Stir through additions.
  3. Freeze overnight.
  4. Eat.
