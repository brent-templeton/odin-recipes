Rich Chocolate Mousse

A super-rich and indulgent Chocolate Mousse that can't be beaten.

Ingredients

  • 500g Dark Chocolate (at least 70% coca solids)
  • 500g Cream (about 32% fat)
  • 250g Sugar
  • 100ml Water
  • 4 Eggs

Method

  1. Bring cream to almost boiling and pour over chopped chocolate. Stir until smooth and creamy. Set aside to cool.
  2. Put sugar and water on to boil in a small saucepan.
  3. Start wisking eggs in a cake mixer until light and fluffy.
  4. When sugar reaches aproxx. 125°C, slowly pour into eggs while still mixing at high speed.
  5. Keep whisking sugar and eggs until lukewarm, about 10-15 mins.
  6. Fold egg mixture through chocolate and cream, taking care to mix well without knocking out the air.
  7. Chill overnight. Serve with grated chocolate.
